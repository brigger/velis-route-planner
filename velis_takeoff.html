Landing correction factors (from POH)

FactorCorrection
HeadwindSubtract 10% per 13 kts headwind
TailwindAdd 10% per 2 kts tailwind (max 10 kts)
Wet grassAdd 30% to ground roll on dry grass
DownslopeAdd 27% of ground roll per 1% downslope
